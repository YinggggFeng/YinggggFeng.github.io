research

Things I think about from time to time

I have broad interests throughout theoretical computer sceince. Currently, I’m particularly interested in algorithms for massive, high-dimensional, or evolving dataset, with security and privacy considerations.

  • One topic that I’m working on is robust streaming algorithms under the white-box adversarial setting, where a stream is chosen adaptively by an adversary who observes the entire internal state of the algorithm at each time step. We showed that nontrivial algorithms are still possible.
